    Qasideh | Kash ki man budami hamrah-i bad-i saba
    Original calligraphy by Ustad Esmaili Mood from Iran inspired by verses 1, 2, 3, 4 and 15 of the qasideh Nawruz-i saʿid amad, ya rab ki mubarak bad.

    Listen to Kash ki man budami hamrah-i bad-i saba:


    TranslationTransliterationLyrics
    I wish I were a companion of the zephyr so that I could pass by the land of Sheba at the time of dawn. Kāsh ki man būdamī hamrah-i bād-i ṣabā
    Tā gudharī kardamī waqt-i saḥar bar sabā 
    کاش که من بودمی همره باد صبا
    تا گذری کردمی وقت سحر بر سبا             
    The letter of the Bilquees of the soul for the Solomon of the heart could not be delivered by anyone but the hoopoe of the zephyr. Nāmah-yi Bilqays-i jān sūy-i Sulaymān-i dil
    Kas narasānad magar Hudhud-i bād-i ṣabā
    نامه‌ی بلقیس جان سوی سلیمانِ دل
    کس نرساند مگر هدهد بادِ صبا             
    If the curls of the beloved’s hair were untied, no one would any longer smell the musk of Hotan. Gar bigushāyad zi ham chīn-i sar-i zulf dūst
    Bīsh nabūyad kasī nāfah-yi mushk-i khatā
    گر بگشايد ز هم چينِ سرِ زلف دوست
    بيش نبويد کسی نافه‌ی مُشکِ ختا             
     
    I am dying in vain and enduring this pain for nothing; this is how I am. Where am I and where is the Beloved? Bīhudah jān mīkanam, khūn-i jigar mīkhuram
    Īn manam ākhir chunīn, dūst kujā man kujā
    بيهده جان می‌کنم خون جگر می‌خورم
    اين منم آخر چنين دوست کجا من کجا             
    Your love was combined with my soul in the pre-eternity. How can separation ever detach me from you? Mihr-i tu bā jān-i man dar azal āmīkhtand
    Hijr marā-u tu rā kay kunad az ham judā
    مهرِ تو با جانِ من در ازل آميختند
    هجر، مرا و تو را کی کند از هم جدا             
     
    Yes, if the jealous ones plotted against me, thank God that this servant is not despondent and hopes for the grace of God. Ārī agar ḥāsidān taʿbīyahʾī sākhtand
    Shukr ki nawmīd nīst bandah zi faḍl-i khudā
    آری اگر حاسدان تعبيه‌ای ساختند
    شکر که نوميد نيست بنده ز فضل خدا             
     
    If he deprives me of this world and my religion, I would not mind. I am content with losing anything but you. War bisitānad zi man dunyī-u dīn bāk nīst
    Bar hamah chīz-i digar ghayr-i tu dāram riḍā
    ور بستاند ز من دنیی و دين، باک نيست
    بر همه چيز دگر غير تو دارم رضا             
     
    You are the Yūsuf of my soul and I am alive with a breath of your scent. How long should I tear my clothes in the anguish of being far from you? Yūsuf-i jānam tuʾī, zindah bi būy-i tuʾam
    Chand kunam pīrhan dar gham-i hijrat qabā
    يوسف جانم تويی، زنده به بوی توام
    چند کنم پیرهن در غم هجرت قبا
