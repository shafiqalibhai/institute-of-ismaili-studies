Speeches of His Highness the Aga Khan IV

His Highness The Aga Khan, the 49th hereditary imam of the Shi‘a Ismaili Muslims, is the founder, and Chairman of the Board of Governors, of The Institute of Ismaili Studies. In this capacity, he guides the Institute’s mandate, strategies, and programmes. His Highness’ vision for the Institute is a part of a larger aspiration that he has expressed for Muslims and the societies in which they live. Over the past four decades, he has articulated various facets of this larger vision in his speeches delivered in varying cultural and geographical settings, a selection from which appears on this website. The speeches must, of course, be understood in their proper historical context.

  2. Closing Address by His Highness the Aga Khan at the “Musée-Musées” Round Table Louvre Museum, October 17, 2007, Paris, France

  3. Address by His Highness the Aga Khan at the Commencement Ceremony of the American University in Cairo, June 15, 2006, Egypt

  4. Address by His Highness the Aga Khan to the Tutzing Evangelical Academy Upon Receiving the "Tolerance" Award, May 20, 2006, Tutzing, Germany

  5. Address by His Highness the Aga Khan to the School of International and Public Affairs, Columbia University, May 15, 2006, New York, USA

  6. Remarks at the International Symposium: “Cosmopolitan Society, Human Safety and Rights in Plural and Peaceful Societies”at the University of Évora, February 12, 2006, Evora, Portugal

  7. Speech at the Foundation Ceremony of the Aga Khan Academy, Maputo, June 25, 2004, Matola, Mozambique

  8. Convocation Address to Graduates of the Ontario Institute for Studies in Education, June 18, 2004, Toronto, Ontario, Canada

  9. Keynote Address at the 2004 Governor General’s Canadian Leadership Conference, May 19, 2004, Quebec, Canada

  13. Speech at the Opening Session of ‘Word of God, Art of Man: The Qur’an and its Creative Expressions’, An International Colloquium organised by The Institute of Ismaili Studies, October 19, 2003, The Ismaili Centre, London

  14. Address at the Foundation Stone Ceremony of The Ismaili Centre, Dushanbe, August 30, 2003, Dushanbe, Tajikistan.

  15. Keynote Speech Concluding the Prince Claus Fund's Conference on Culture and Development, September 7, 2002, Amsterdam

  16. Remarks at the Opening of the Smithsonian Folklife Festival, June 26, 2002, Washington, DC, USA.

  17. Speech at the Banquet Hosted in Honour of Governor Perry, June 23, 2002, Houston, Texas, USA.

  18. Speech at the Inauguration of the Ismaili Jamatkhana and Center, Houston, June 23, 2002, Houston, Texas, USA.

  19. Speech at the 2001 Award Presentation Ceremony of The Aga Khan Award For Architecture, November 6, 2001, The Citadel, Aleppo, Syria.
