    Alumni Services

    As well as acting as your main point of contact with the Institute once you have completed your programme, the Alumni Relations Unit at the IIS offers a number of benefits and services including:

    • Regional Chapter Groups: access to the IIS alumni association, wherever you are in the world. Currently, the Institute has 3 volunteer-run chapter groups in Asia, Europe and North America;
    • The Internship Programme – helping new graduates to gain experience within the institutions of the Aga Khan Development Network and the Ismaili community as well as relevant external agencies and institutions;
    • Research Grants – supporting alumni who wish to pursue further research, present papers at academic conferences, publish articles, or undertake field studies in areas of relevance to the IIS’ mandate;
    • Access to the IIS Library and other facilities;
    • Annual Newsletters
    • Events - Annual Academic Seminars, Reunions held triennially, regular Chapter Group meetings and other scholarly events
    • Careers guidance and development
    • Access to potential professional opportunities at the IIS and AKDN and volunteering opportunities.

    We are continually working to improve the services we provide to the alumni. We hope that you will stay in touch and use this site. Please contact us on alumni@iis.ac.uk or visit us during office hours on the fifth floor of the IIS’ premises in Central London.
