    Qasideh | Ghayib nashud zamani az khatiram khiyalat 
    Original calligraphy by Ustad Esmaili Mood from Iran of verses 1, 3, 4 and 6 from qasideh Ghayib nashud zamani az khatiram khiyalat. 

    Listen to Ghayib nashud zamani az khatiram khiyalat:


    TranslationTransliterationLyrics
    Your image never left my memory; I am keeping the mirror of your beauty constantly before me.Ghāyib nashud zamānī az khāṭiram khīyālat
    Dar pīsh-i chashm dāram āʾīnah-yi jamālat
    غايب نشد زمانی از خاطرم خيالت
    در پيش چشم دارم آيينه‌ی جمالت             
    Seeing you every morning is a blessing; blessed is the one who has the privilege of seeing you to his content. Har bāmdād dīdan-i rūyat khujastah bāshad
    Farrukh kasī ki bīnad bar khīshtan bi fālat
    هر بامداد ديدن رویت خجسته باشد
    فرّخ کسی که بيند بر خويشتن به فالت             
    We are bowing down in obedience and looking at the door in expectation until we are given permission to reunite with you. Sar bar khaṭ-i irādat dārim-u dīdah bar dar
    Tā kay dahand mā rā parwānah-yi wiṣālat
    سر بر خط ارادت داريم و ديده بر در
    تا کی دهند ما را پروانه‌ی وصالت             
    Let all hearts be sacrificed at your feet and all souls be sacrificed upon hearing your Name. Even if you spill our blood, all is permissible for you. Dilhā nithār-i pāyat, jānhā fidāy-i nāmat
    Gar khūn-i mā birīzī, īn nīz ham halālat
    دل‌ها نثار پایت، جان‌ها فدای نامت
    گر خون ما بريزی اين نيز هم حلالت             
    Even Jesus Christ dare not breathe with the miracle of your Speech; even Moses would cover his eyes in the brightness of your Glorious Majesty. ʿĪsā nafas nayārad bā muʿjiz-i kalāmat
    Mūsā naẓar bidūzad az partaw-i jalālat
    عيسی نفس نيارد با معجز کلام‌ات
    موسی نظر بدوزد از پرتو جلالت             
     
    When you are enraged, come and blame us; do not ruin an entire world when you are angry with us. Waqtī ki khashmnākī bā mā ʿitāb mīkun
    Bar ham mazan jahānī kaz mā buwad malālat
    وقتی که خشم‌ناکی با ما عتاب می‌کن
    بر هم مزن جهانی کز ما بُود ملالت             
     
    I said I belong to you alone. I have divorced myself so that I may become annihilated in your perfect Existence. Guftam hamah tu rā-am, khud rā ṭalāq dādam
    Tā bū ki maḥw gardam dar hastī-yi kamālat
    گفتم همه تو را-ام، خود را طلاق دادم
    تا بو که محو گردم در هستی کمالت             
     
    You said you have no determination. How long will you simply make claims, O Nizārī? Enough of all this! Your words and utterances cannot convince us! Guftā qadam nadārī tā kay dam ay Nizārī
    Bas kun ki dar nagīrad bā mā bi qīl-u qālat
    گفتا قدم نداری، تا کی دم ای نزاری
    بس کن که در نگيرد به ما به قيل و قالت
