    Additions to the Site: 2005

    Visitors are invited to register their email address to receive priority information about site updates.

    December 2005

    • News story about the Class of 2008.
    • New Gallery page: Music and Poetry from the Pamir Mountains
    • News story about the first Seminar on Career Pathways for IIS Graduates.
    • Introductions added to academic publications:
      • Muslim Ethics: Emerging Vistas
      • Paradise of Submission: A Medieval Treatise on Ismaili Thought
      • Memoirs of a Mission: The Ismaili Scholar, Statesman and Poet, al-Mu’ayyad fi’l-Din al-Shirazi
      • The Advent of the Fatimids: A Contemporary Shi'i Witness
      • Beauty and Islam: Aesthetics in Islamic Art and Architecture
      • Civil Society in the Muslim World: Contemporary Perspectives
      • Ismaili Literature: A Bibliography of Sources and Studies
    • New Arabic manuscript added: the Muhaj al-da'awat wa-manhaj al-'inayat and al-Mujtana min al-du'a' al-mujtaba of Ibn Tawus

    November 2005

    • News story about the first meetings of the North American and European Chapter Groups of the IIS Alumni Association.
    • New article in the Lifelong Learning pages: Reflective Learnings From The Training Programmes of the Ismaili Muslim Conciliation and Arbitration Boards, Globally by Mohamed M. Keshavjee and Tony Whatling.

    October 2005

    • News story about Professor Azim Nanji's speech “Rethinking the Muslim World in a Global Perspective” at Stanford University

    September 2005

    • News story about the 2005 PhD Scholarship Awards
    • New article in the Encyclopaedia Contributions pages: ‘Dezkuh’ by Farhad Daftary
    • New article in the Lifelong Learning pages: Andalusia, the Shrine of the Revealed Faiths by Alnoor Dhanani.
    • News story about the Institute’s annual Summer Programme on Islam at the University of Cambridge

    August 2005

    • News story about a Research Methodology Course conducted by the IIS in Tajikistan
    • News story about the IIS Library's inauguration of the Annemarie Schimmel Memorial Reference Collection.
    • New Arabic manuscript added: the Dala'il al-khayrat of Muhammad ibn Sulayman al-Jazuli
    • News story about publication of the Proceedings of the Nasir Khusraw Millenary Conference (2003).

    July 2005

    • New article in the Lifelong Learning pages: “Ismaili Studies: Background and Modern Developments” by Dr Farhad Daftary.
    • News story about The International Islamic Conference, held in Amman, Jordan from July 4 to 6, 2005.
    • Two new articles in the Lifelong Learning pages: Documents of History, Symbols of Authority: The Coinage of the Fatimids by Alnoor Merchant and Civil Imagination after September 11, 2001 by Amyn B. Sajoo.
    • News story about a Workshop for Institute of Ismaili Studies Alumni from 24th-30th June, in London.

    June 2005

    • News story about a paper at the ‘Moses Maimonides: Bridging the Divide,’ seminar: ‘Revisiting Maimonides’ Critique of kalam’ by Dr Alnoor Dhanani.
    • New gallery page: Reflections on the first 25 Years
    • News story about the first IIS publication available in Turkish: a translation by Muhammet Seviker of Intellectual Traditions in Islam, edited by Dr Farhad Daftary.
    • Two new Arabic manuscripts added: Ibrahim ibn al-Husayn al-Hamidi's Diya' al-hulum and 'Ali ibn Hanzala's Kanz al-walad
    • New article in the Academic Papers pages: Philosophical remarks on Scripture by Aziz Esmail

    May 2005

    • News story about the latest publication supported by the Institute and published by I.B. Tauris: Ismaili Literature: A Bibliography of Sources and Studies by Dr Daftary’s
    • New article in the Lifelong Learning pages: “Islam’s Reformist Tradition” by Abdou Filali-Ansary
    • New article in the Lifelong Learning pages: Introductory Notes on the Quran
    • News story about Professor Azim Nanji leading a discussion about diversity and religion at ‘Diversity: a stronger economy, a better Britain’, March, 2005, London.
    • News story about the latest publication supported by the Institute and published by I.B. Tauris: Crossing the Threshold: Understanding Religious Identities in South Asia by Dominique-Sila Khan

    April 2005

    • New article in the Academic Papers pages: “Sayyida Hurra: The Isma‘ili Sulayhid Queen of Yemen” by Farhad Daftary
    • New Arabic manuscript added: The Hashiyah 'ala al-muhakamat of Habib Allah al-Shirazi
    • New article in the Encyclopaedia Contributions pages: ‘Hasan Sabbah’ by Farhad Daftary
    • New article in the Encyclopaedia Contributions pages: ‘Hasan II’ by Farhad Daftary
    • New Gallery page: Wellsprings of Wisdom: Persian Contributions to Ismaili Thought

    March 2005

    • News story about Professor Azim Nanji speaking on Islam in the 21st Century at Berkhamsted Collegiate School
    • New Arabic manuscript added: Abu Ya‘qub al-Sijistani’s Kitab al- yanabi‘
    • News story about the latest publication in the Ismaili Texts and Translations Series: Paradise of Submission: A Medieval Treatise on Ismaili Thought, edited and translated by S.J. Badakhchani
    • New article in the Lifelong Learning pages: Evolution of the Shi‘a Ismaili Tradition in Central Asia by Sarfaroz Niyozov.

    February 2005

    • News story about the Graduating Class of 2005 .
    • News story about Dr. Farhad Daftary speaking on ‘Shi‘ism and the Ismailis in History’ at the ‘Understanding Islam: A Conference on the Culture and Religion of Islam’ at The Dublin Castle Conference Centre, 6-8 December, 2004.
    • New article in the Academic Papers pages: “Muslim Saints and Hindu Rulers: The Development of Sufi and Ismaili Mysticism in the Non-Muslim States of India” by Dominique-Sila Khan.
    • News story about Dr. Farhad Daftary's lecture on “The Persian Contributions to Ismaili History and Literature” in a series of annual lectures in Iranian Studies at Simon Fraser Universit, November 2004.

    January 2005

    • News story about the speech "Philanthropy in Islam" given at the International Charity Law Comparative Seminar in Beijing, China, October, 2004.
    • News story about the IIS Workshop on Community and Civic Life: New Directions in the Study of the Ismaili Living Traditions of South Asia.
    • News story about the IIS Panel ( Defining without Confining: Reflections on Prophetic Usage of Sacred Space ) at the 38th Annual Meeting
      of MESA.
