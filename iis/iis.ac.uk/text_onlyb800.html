    Qasideh | Khiz ki imruz jahan an-i mast
    Original calligraphy by Ustad Esmaili Mood from Iran inspired by qasideh Khiz ki imruz jahan an-i mast.

    Listen to Khiz ki imruz jahan an-i mast:


    TranslationTransliterationLyrics
    Lo! Rise! Today the entire universe belongs to us! The soul of the entire universe is now our cupbearer and our guest! Khīz ki imrūz jahān ān-i māst
    Jān-u jahān sāqīy-u mihmān-i māst 
    خیز که امروز جهان آنِ ماست
    جان و جهان ساقی و مهمانِ ماست             
    In the heart and the afreet and the fairies, it is the glory of our Solomon reigning. Dar dil-u dar dīdah-yi dīw-u parī
    Dabdabah-yi farr-i Sulaymān-i māst
    در دل و در دیده‌ی دیو و پری
    دبدبه‌ی فرّ سلیمانِ ماست             
    The great Rustam and thousands like him are now servants and toys in our game. Rustam-i dastān-u hizārān chu ū
    Bandah-u bāzīchah-yi dastān-i māst
    رستم دستان و هزاران چو او
    بنده و بازیچه‌ی دستانِ ماست             
     
    Is it not enough pride and privilege for my Egypt that its ruler is our Joseph of Canaan? Bas nabuwad Miṣr-i marā īn sharaf
    Īn ki shahash Yūsuf-i Kanʿān-i māst
    بس نبود مصر مرا این شرف
    این که شه‌اش یوسف کنعانِ ماست             
    Rise! Because the commander of the soul and the universe is now gracefully following our command! Khīz ki farmāndih-i jān-u jahān
    Az karam imrūz bi farmān-i māst
    خیز که فرمان‌دهِ جان و جهان
    از کرم امروز به فرمانِ ماست             
     
    The King who bestows kingship upon kings is our musician today; our fairy-faced beloved is now summoning all our fairies. Shāh-i shahībakhsh ṭarabsāz-i māst
    Yār-i parī rūy parī khān-i māst
    شاهِ شهی‌بخش طرب ساز ماست
    یار پری‌روی پری‌خوانِ ماست             
     
    That Lord who is the pride of the polo and the club is thankfully in our field. Ān malik-i mafkhar-i chawgān-u gūy
    Shukr ki imrūz bi maydān-i māst
    آن ملک مفخر چوگان و گوی
    شکر که امروز به میدانِ ماست             
     
    That Lord of the territory of the soul and the heart is now residing in our distressed soul and heart. Ān malik-i mamlikat-i jān-u dil
    Dar dil-u dar jān-i parīshān-i māst
    آن ملک مملکت جان و دل
    در دل و در جان پریشانِ ماست             
     
    Who is that fellow curling up in sorrow in the corner of the heart? Pull him in as he is the mine of all sweetness for us! Kīst dar ān gūshah-yi dil tan zadah
    Pīsh kashash kū shikaristān-i māst
    کیست در آن گوشه دل تن زده
    پیش کشش کو شکرستانِ ماست             
     
    The treasurer of paradise who is the moon of paradise is now intoxicated with satisfaction with our satisfying beloved. Khāzin-i riḍwān ki mah-i jannat ast
    Mast-i riḍāy-i dil-i riḍwān-i māst
    خازن رضوان که مه جنت‌ است
    مست رضای دل رضوانِ ماست             
     
    He has stirred this flavour in us and gone into hiding; he is the salt of our life and our saltshaker. Shūr dar afkandah-u pinhān shudah
    Ū namak-i ʿumr-u namakdān-i māst
    شور درافکنده و پنهان شده
    او نمک عمر و نمکدانِ ماست             
     
    He has gone to a corner and the entire universe is intoxicated with him; he is our Khiḍr and the spring of eternal life. Gushah giriftast-u jahān mast-i ūst
    Ū khaḍir-u chashmih-yi ḥaywān-i māst
    گوشه گرفتست و جهان مست اوست
    او خضر و چشمه‌ی حیوان ماست             
     
    He is like the salt of the pot of our meal and he is like the soul in the body; he is more manifest than all and yet hidden from us. Chun namak-i dīg-u chu jān dar badan
    Az hamah ẓāhirtar-u pinhān-i māst
    چون نمک دیگ و چو جان در بدن
    از همه ظاهرتر و پنهانِ ماست             
     
    He appears to be non-existent and he is the entire being himself; it is only us in existence when he belongs to us. Nīstnamāyandah-u khud jumlah ūst
    Khud hamah māʾīm chu ū ān-i māst
    نیست‌نماینده و خود جمله اوست
    خود همه ماییم چو او آنِ ماست             
     
    Stop bringing proofs and reasons because love is our only proof and reason in silence. Bīsh magū ḥujjat-u burhān ki ʿishq
    Dar khamushī ḥujjat-u burhān-i māst
    بیش مگو حجت و برهان که عشق
    در خمشی حجت و برهانِ ماست
