    Qasideh | Nawruz-i saʿid amad, ya rab ki mubarak bad
    Original calligraphy by Ustad Esmaili Mood from Iran inspired by verses 3, 4, 10 and 12 from qasideh Nawruz-i saʿid amad, ya rab ki mubarak bad.

    Listen to Nawruz-i saʿid amad, ya rab ki mubarak bad:


    TranslationTransliterationLyrics
    Happy Nawrūz has arrived; O Lord! May it be blessed! Purity has come in abundance; O Lord! May it be blessed!. Nawrūz-i saʿīd āmad, yā rab ki mubārak bād
    Nuzhat bi mazīd āmad, yā rab ki mubārak bād
    نوروز سعيد آمد، يارب که مبارک باد
    نُزهت به مزيد آمد، يارب که مبارک باد             
    The world has once again become illuminated and has become the ornament of the land; the wine is restless to be in the cup; O Lord! May it be blessed! ʿĀlam zi naw anwar shud, ārāyish-i kishwar shud
    May rāghib-i sāghar shud, yā rab ki mubārak bād
    عالم ز نو انور شد، آرايش کشور شد
    می راغب ساغر شد، يارب که مبارک باد             
    The garden of songs has become filled with flowers; the cup of the heart is filled with wine; the nightingale of the soul is singing happily; O Lord! May it be blessed! Shud bāgh-i ṭarab pur gul, shud shīshay-i dil pur mul
    Zad bulbul-i jān ghulghul, yā rab ki mubārak bād
    شد باغ طرب پر گل،‌ شد شيشه‌ی دل پر مُل
    زد بلبل جان غلغل، يارب که مبارک باد             
     
    Frustrations have now departed with divine Mercy; the world has become resplendent; O Lord! May it be blessed! Bugdhasht parīshānī az raḥmat-i rabbānī
    ʿĀlam shuda nūrānī yā rab ki mubārak bād
    بگذشت پريشانی، از رحمت ربانی
    عالم شده نورانی، يارب که مبارک باد             
    The garden is flourishing and growing; the home is full of light and purity; the lilies have opened up their tongues; O Lord! May it be blessed! Dar nashw-u namā gulshan, pur nūr-u ṣafā maskan
    Bugshādah zabān sūsan, yā rab ki mubārak bād
    در نشو و نما گلشن، پر نور و صفا مسکن
    بگشاده زبان سوسن، يارب که مبارک باد             
     
    The drum rolls of celebration have arrived; felicitations have become manifest; the happiness of hearts has arrived; O Lord! May it be blessed! Manshūr-i surūr āmad, ʿishrat bi ẓuhūr āmad
    Tafrīḥ-ī ṣudūr āmad, yā rab ki mubārak bād
    منشور سرور آمد، عشرت به ظهور آمد
    تفريح صدور آمد، يارب که مبارک باد             
     
    Winter’s bitterness has come to an end; the heart is now aware of Spring; every moment say a hundred times: O Lord! May it be blessed! Shud shiddat-i day kūtah, dil shud zi rabīʿ āgah
    Har laḥẓah bigū ṣad rah, yā rab ki mubārak bād
    شد شدت دی کوته، دل شد ز ربيع آگه
    هر لحظه بگو صد ره،‌ يارب که مبارک باد             
     
    All means of happiness are available in abundance, for all those whether present or absent; the mind is free from pains; O Lord! May it be blessed! Asbāb-i khushū wāfir, ham ghāyib-u ham ḥāḍir
    Fārigh zi alam khāṭir, yā rab ki mubārak bād
    اسباب خوشی وافر، هم غايب و هم حاضر
    فارغ ز الم خاطر، يارب که مبارک باد             
     
    All pains and troubles are resolved and the rope of injustice severed; the jealous have become miserable; O Lord! May it be blessed! Shud ranj-u alam chārah, shud ḥabl-i sitam pārah
    Ḥāsid shudah bīchārah, yā rab ki mubārak bād
    شد رنج و الم چاره، شد حبل ستم پاره
    حاسد شده بيچاره، يارب که مبارک باد             
     
    The veil has been lifted from the way and the heart is now filled with certainty; the nightingale of the soul is now singing; O Lord! May it be blessed! Barkhāst ḥijāb az rah, dil shud zi yaqīn āgah
    Zad bulbul-i jān chahchah, yā rab ki mubārak bād
    برخاست حجاب از ره، دل شد ز يقين آگه
    زد بلبل جان چهچه، يارب که مبارک باد             
     
    The sweeper of the zephyr has cleared hearts of pain; celebrations are now coupled with purity; O Lord! May it be blessed! Farrāsh-i ṣabā har dam afshāndah zi dilhā gham
    ʿIshrat bi ṣafā hamdam, yā rab ki mubārak bād
    فراش صبا هر دم، افشانده ز دل‌ها غم
    عشرت به صفا همدم، يارب که مبارک باد             
     
    The desert has now been decked with the silk of flowers; the world has become like a paradise; O Lord! May it be blessed! Bar qāmat-i khud ṣaḥrā, pūshīda zi dībā gul
    Shud dahr bihisht āsa, yā rab ki mubārak bād
    بر قامت خود صحرا، پوشيده ز ديبا گل
    شد دهر بهشت‌آسا، يارب که مبارک باد             
     
    Creator and Crafter! O All-Powerful with authority! You are both the Living and the Adjudicator! O Lord! May it be blessed! Ay ṣāniʿ-i ṣanʿatgar, ay qādir-i qudratwar
    Ham ḥayyī-u ham dāwar, yā rab ki mubārak bād
    ای صانع صنعت‌گر، ای قادر قدرت‌ور
    هم حیّ‌ای و هم داور، يارب که مبارک باد             
     
    You are the Compassionate and the Error-Concealing One! You are the Forgiver and the Ruling One! You are the One who conceals sins and forgives them; O Lord! May it be blessed! Tu rāḥim-u sattārī, tu ghāfir-u jabbārī
    Tu sātir-u ghaffārī, yā rab ki mubārak bād
    تو راحم و ستاری، تو غافر و جباری
    تو ساتر و غفاری، يارب که مبارک باد             
     
    You are both Manifest and Hidden; you are both Present and Absent; You are both the Protector and the Helper; O Lord! May it be blessed! Ham bāṭin-u ẓahir tu, ham ghāyib-u ḥāḍir tu
    Ham ḥāfiẓ-u nāṣir tu, yā rab ki mubārak bād
    هم باطن و ظاهر تو، هم غايب و حاضر تو
    هم حافظ و ناصر تو، يارب که مبارک باد
     
    You are the One with blessed Grace and it is through Your Grace the soul lives; the heart submits to Your Command; O Lord! May it be blessed! Ay luṭf-i tu farkhundah, waz luṭf-i tu jan zindah
    Dil amr-i tu rā bandah, yā rab ki mubārak bād
    ای لطف تو فرخنده، وز لطف تو جان زنده
    دل امر تو را بنده، يارب که مبارک باد             
     
    You are the purpose of the sharīʿat; You are the destination of the ṭarīqat; You are the Lord of ḥaqīqat; O Lord! May it be blessed! Maṭlab zi sharīʿat tu, maqṣad zi ṭarīqat tu
    Sulṭan-i ḥaqīqat tu, yā rab ki mubārak bād
    مطلب ز شريعت تو، مقصد ز طريقت تو
    سلطان حقيقت تو، يارب که مبارک باد             
     
    You are the One whose name is the protector of the soul and the heart is trembling from Your Wrath; the soul is a servant of Your Rewards; O Lord! May it be blessed! Ay ḥirz-i rawān nāmat, dil khāyif-i ibrāmat
    Jān banda-yi inʿāmat, yā rab ki mubārak bād
    ای حرز روان نامت، دل خايف ابرامت
    جان بنده‌ی اِنعامت، يارب که مبارک باد             
     
    Poor Ḥusayn pledges loyalty to You from the depth of his heart; this is his very religion; O Lord! May it be blessed! Bīchārah Ḥusayn az jān bandad bi ghamat paymān
    Īn ast warā īmān, yā rab ki mubārak bād
    بيچاره حسين از جان، بندد به غمت پيمان
    این است ورا ايمان، یارب که مبارک باد             
     
    As the glad tiding of ʿĪd arrived, the beginning came to an opening; Happy Nawrūz has arrived; O Lord! May it be blessed! Chu muzhda-yi ʿīd āmad, mabdaʾ bi muʿīd āmad
    Nawrūz-i saʿīd āmad, yā rab ki mubārak bād
    چون مژده‌ی عيد آمد، مبداء به معيد آمد
    نوروز سعيد آمد، يارب که مبارک باد
