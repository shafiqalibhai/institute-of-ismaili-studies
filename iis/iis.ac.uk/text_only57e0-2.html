    Additions to the Site: 2001

    Visitors are invited to register their email address to receive priority information about site updates.

    December 2001

    • The Amirali Mamdani Collection: A small selection of images from a large collection of original slides, photographs and films.
    • New article added to the Academic Papers section: “The Metaphysics of Interfaith Dialogue: A Qur’anic Perspective” by Dr Reza Shah-Kazemi adapted his presentation at “Paths to the Heart: Sufism and the Christian East” Conference
    • New Gallery added: Coinage of the Fatimids.
    • New article added to the Academic Papers section: “Towards New Horizons in Islamic Studies” adapted from “Publications from the Institute of Ismaili Studies: Towards New Horizons in Islamic Studies,” by Dr Aziz Esmail.
    • News story about The Institute of Ismaili Studies participation in the 35th Annual Meeting of the Middle Eastern Studies Association of North America (MESA), held from November 17-20, 2001 in San Francisco, California.
    • New Gallery added: The Calligraphic Tradition in Islam, adapted from a presentation by Dr Duncan Haldane at “The Spirit of Islam: Experiencing Islam through Calligraphy”
    • Two new articles added to the Lifelong Learning section: “Religion and Globalisation: Perspectives for the New Millennium”, and “The Institutions of the Ismaili Muslim Community”, both by Shams Vellani

    November 2001

    • New articles added to the Lifelong Learning section: “What is the Contribution of Religions to Peace?” by Shams Vellani and “Beyond the Clash of Civilizations” by Professor Azim Nanji
    • Graduate Studies Lectures and DARP Seminars pages updated.

    • Speech by His Highness The Aga Khan at the 2001 Award Presentation Ceremony of The Aga Khan Award For Architecture, November 6, 2001, The Citadel, Aleppo, Syria.

    October 2001

    • News story about The Institute’s newest publication, Doctrines of Shi‘i Islam: A Compendium of Imami Beliefs and Practices translated and edited by Dr Reza Shah-Kazemi from the Persian original by Ayatollah Ja‘far Sobhani
    • The Class of 2004 added to Current Students lists.
    • Story on the Graduating Class of 2002
    • News story about the final seminar in the “Civil Society Series” with an exploration of “Civic Culture in ‘Secular’ Turkey” by Professor Ersin Kalay&ccedilioglu.

    September 2001

    • News story about Class of 2003 students studying Arabic in Morocco
    • Seminars, Conferences and Public Lectures page updated
    • Story on the Workshop for ITREB personnel held at the Institute
    • Gallery of Images from Beauty and Islam: Aesthetics in Islamic Art and Architecture with additional photographs by the author
    • News story about two new IIS publications: Beauty and Islam: Aesthetics in Islamic Art and Architecture by Valérie Gonzalez and Struggling with the Philosopher: A Refutation of Avicenna’s Metaphysics by Wilferd Madelung and Tobias Mayer
    • Academic Publications and New Publications lists updated
    • Information about Faculty & Staff updated
    • Updated Ismaili Heritage Series and Ismaili Texts and Translations pages

    August 2001

    • More pictures added to Image Gallery of the Annual Seminar in Tajikistan
    • News story about the Institute of Ismaili Studies participation in the conference of the British Society for Middle Eastern Studies (BRISMES)
    • Additions to Forthcoming Events and the Civil Society Seminar list pages.
    • Updated Glossary of terms and their definitions.
      • Updated Research Programmes and Forthcoming Publications pages
    • New Translations pages added to the Publications section

    July 2001

    • News story about Annual Seminar in Tajikistan on Faith and Intellect, organised by the IIS’ Project Tajikistan in collaboration with the Tajik Academy of Sciences
    • Image Gallery of the Annual Seminar in Tajikistan
    • News story about IIS Governor Professor Afzal Ahmed's lecture “The Mutual Influence of Learning and the Development of Mathematical Thinking in the Arab Civilisation during 900-1200 AD” at the conference “Custodian, Creator and Communicator: Arabic Mathematics and Science in the period 700-1200”, organised by the Norwegian National Commission for UNESCO in association with the Norwegian Council for Mathematics
    • Information about Faculty & Staff updated

    June 2001

    • Forthcoming Events updated with IIS involvement at Annual Meeting of the British Middle Eastern Studies Association (BRISMES), July, 2001 and at the Annual Meeting of the Middle Eastern Studies Association (MESA), November, 2001
    • News story about a full-day event as part of the Institute’s “Civil Society in Muslim Contexts” Series; three speakers included Dr Norani Othman on Southeast Asia, Dr Ziba Mir-Hosseini on gender in post-revolutionary Iran; and Professor Abdou Filali-Ansary on the Maghreb
    • Information about four IIS books, newly available in paperback
    • Information about Faculty & Staff updated; new biographical notes added

    May 2001

    • The 5th session in the “Civil Society in Muslim Contexts” Series: “The Good Society: An Ethical Perspective” by Professor Azim Nanji
    • A new page of Forthcoming Events
    • Updated information about the Institute's Seminars, Conferences and Public Lectures, including the Department of Academic Research and Publications Seminars and the Graduate Studies Lecture
    • News story about an honorary degree awarded to Professor Mohamed Arkoun by The University of Exeter
    • Abstract of Professor Arkoun's keynote Address at the “Orientalism Reconsidered” Conference
    • Transcript of a Speech by His Highness the Aga Khan at the Centenary Celebration Meeting, Association of American Universities
    • Remarks by His Highness the Aga Khan at the White House Conference on Culture and Diplomacy

    April 2001

    • News story about Professor Azim Nanji's lecture at the University of Waterloo on “Beyond the Clash of Civilizations”
    • A new page of news relating to His Highness the Aga Khan was opened with a story about the White House Conference on Culture and Diplomacy
    • The latest version of IIS Update

    March 2001

    • The 4th session in the “Civil Society in Muslim Contexts” Series: “Pakistan: Between Identity Politics and Civil Society” by Dr Iftikhar Malik

    February 2001

    • The 3rd session in the “Civil Society in Muslim Contexts” Series: “Civic Identity in the New Central Asia” by Dr Olivier Roy
    • Information about Faculty & Staff updated
    • The second lecture of the "Civil Society in Muslim Contexts" Series: "Tajikistan: From Civil War to Civil Society?" by Dr Shirin Akiner

    January 2001

    • Transcript of a speech by His Highness the Aga Khan at the conference on Indigenous Philanthropy (October 17, 2000, Islamabad, Pakistan)
    • Updated information about Current Students of the Graduate Programme in Islamic Studies and Humanities
