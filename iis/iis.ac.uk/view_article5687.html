    Alumni Research Grant

    Terms of Reference:
    The Institute of Ismaili Studies has established an Alumni Research Grant. The purpose of this grant is to support alumni who wish to pursue further research, present papers at academic conferences, publish articles, or undertake field studies in areas of relevance to the IIS. Each application will be considered on an individual basis by an Alumni Grant Committee, appointed by the IIS and comprising four of its scholars, and their recommendations will be reviewed by the IIS Co-Directors who will make the final decision.
     
    Each grant will be for an amount not exceeding £1,000 for field research/publication and not exceeding £500 for presenting papers at academic conferences. The deadline for applications will be the 15th of March, June and September of each year. Applications received during the preceding quarter will be reviewed together to ensure that the grant is allocated competitively. Any individual who has received a grant is not eligible to re-apply for a period of five years.

    Conditions:

    · The award is made towards the named alumnus/na conducting field research, presenting at a conference or publishing.

    · The alumnus/na is required to supply the Alumni Relations Unit with an abstract/report, along with an assessment of the contribution made by the alumnus/na, together with a final statement of expenditure, within one month of the conclusion of the project/conference.
