    IIS Update


    Beginning in the year 2000, The Institute of Ismaili Studies began to publish the annual IIS Update to inform its supporters, affiliates and well-wishers of its programmes and activities over the previous year. Also included in the Update is information about the Institute’s recent and forthcoming publications as well as news about its faculty and staff.
     
    All of the Updates are available for download as PDFs. In order to keep the file sizes as small as possible, we have not included high-resolution images: some of the images may not appear very sharp. If you would like a printed copy of the IIS Update, please send us a request at the address provided on the Contact Us page.
