IIS Publishes A Modern History of the Ismailis 
April 2011

This publication is the culmination of the most recent scholarship, in many cases based on field research, as well as oral traditions and community documents not published hitherto. As such, it is the first publication of its kind dealing with a modern history of the Ismailis. More
