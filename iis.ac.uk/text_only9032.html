    Additions to the Site: 2003

    Visitors are invited to register their email address to receive priority information about site updates.

      December 2003

      • Story about Professor Azim Nanji, who has been invited by Stanford University’s Department of Religious Studies to be a Visiting Professor of Islamic Studies for its Winter and Spring terms.
      • New and updated reading lists added to the Learning section.
      • Story about His Highness the Aga Khan announcing the first Ismaili Centre to be built in the Middle East at a Foundation Laying Ceremony for the new centre in Dubai, United Arab Emirates.
      • Story about the The Institute’s project to publish a complete critical edition and annotated English translation of the Rasa’il Ikhwan al-Safa’ (The Epistles of the Brethren of Purity).
      • Address by His Highness the Aga Khan to 2003 Aga Khan University Convocation, December 6, 2003, Karachi, Pakistan.
      • Story about the The Institute’s latest publication in the Ismaili Heritage Series: Memoirs of a Mission: The Ismaili Scholar, Statesman and Poety, al-Mu’ayyad fi’l-din al-Shirazi by Verena Klemm.
      • New Academic article: “Between Metaphor and Context: The Nature of the Fatimid Ismaili Discourse on Justice and Injustice” by Azim Nanji.
      • A new gallery of unidentified pictures called Picture of the Week based on selected images from the Institute's various collections
      • Story about His Highness the Aga Khan, Chancellor of the Aga Khan University at the institution’s 16th Convocation in Karachi, Pakistan.

      November 2003

      • Story about ten students from five countries who were accepted into the Institute’s Graduate Programme in Islamic Studies and Humanities (the Class of 2006)
      • A new Gallery entitled Nizari Ismaili Castles of Iran and Syria based on selected images is based on the ongoing research of Peter Willey
      • Story about the The Institute’s latest publication, Culture and Memory in Medieval Islam, edited by Farhad Daftary and Josef W. Meri, is a festschrift honouring the contributions of Wilferd Madelung
      • Story about the three-day international colloquium, “Word of God, Art of Man: The Qur’an and its Creative Expressions”, launched on October 18, 2003
      • New Lifelong Learning article: ‘The Classification of the Sciences according to the Rasa’il Ikhwan al-Safa’ by Godefroid de Callataÿ.
      • Director’s Address by Azim Nanji and Valedictory Address by Sabrina Datoo, from the 25th Anniversary Graduation Ceremony, October 19, 2003

      October 2003

      • Story about the series of events organised to commemorate the 25th Anniversary of The Institute of Ismaili Studies
      • Story about the three-day international colloquium, “Word of God, Art of Man: The Qur’an and its Creative Expressions”, launched on October 18, 2003
      • Story about the graduation ceremony in London for the Class of 2004 and honouring the alumni of the Institute’s varied programmes; guests included the Chairman of the Institute’s Board of Governors, His Highness the Aga Khan.
      • A new Gallery entitled Illuminating Beauty, Illustrating Harmony: A Gallery celebrating the 25th Anniversary of The Institute of Ismaili Studies, based on the exhibition held at the Isaili Centre, London 18th - 26th October, 2003
      September 2003
      • Story about the series of events organised by the IIS to commemorate the Millennium Birth Anniversary of Nasir Khusraw
      • Story about the first book published by The Institute of Ismaili Studies in Russian: Kratkaya istoriya isma‘ilizma, a translation of A Short History of the Ismailis by Farhad Daftary
      • Story about the launch of The Ismaili Centre by His Highness the Aga Khan on August 30, 2003, in Dushanbe, Tajikistan
      • Two new articles in the Encyclopaedia Contributions pages: ‘al-Hakim bi-Amr Allah’ by Farhad Daftary and ‘Ismaili Communities - South Asia’ by Farhad Daftary and Azim Nanji
      • Address by His Highness the Aga Khan at the Foundation Stone Ceremony of The Ismaili Centre, Dushanbe.

      August 2003

      • Story about the Second IIS ‘Summer Programme on Islam’, held at the University of Cambridge
      • Story about Professor Azim Nanji Addressing the ‘Diversity in Islam’ Conference in Ottawa
      • Story about the 2003 Doctoral Scholarships awarded, and this year’s three recipients
      • New Life Long Learning article: “A Changing Religious Landscape: Perspectives on the Muslim Experience in North America” by Azim Nanji.

      July 2003

      • Five new articles from the The Internet Encyclopedia of Philosophy in the Encyclopaedia Contributions pages.
      • Story about the collection of books, belonging to the late Annemarie Schimmel, which has been donated to the Institute’s Library in her memory.
      • New Life Long Learning article: “Arbitration and Mediation in the Shi‘a Imami Ismaili Muslim Community” by Mohamed M. Keshavjee.
      • Five new articles from the Encyclopaedia Iranica in the Encyclopaedia Contributions pages.

      June 2003

      • Story about the newly published Arabic Ismaili Manuscripts: The Zahid ‘Ali Collection in The Library of The Institute of Ismaili Studies by Delia Cortese
      • Story about Professor Azim Nanji's address to the meeting of The International Baccalaureate Organization (IBO).

      May 2003

      • New Gallery: Manuscripts in the Zahid ‘Ali Collection.
      • New Life Long Learning article: “Reason and Religion: The Old Argument Revisited”, by Aziz Esmail.
      • The glossary expanded by 164 terms.
      • Story about the Institute of Ismaili Studies collaboration with The Center for the Great Islamic Encyclopedia (Markaz Da’irat al-Ma‘arif-e Buzurg-e Islami) in Tehran.

      April 2003

      • New Life Long Learning article: “The Music of Tajik Badakhshan”, by Jan van Belle.
      • New Life Long Learning article: “Intellect and Religion, East and West”, by Reza Shah-Kazemi.
      • New Life Long Learning article: “The Pleasures of ‘Islamic’ Art,” by Amyn B. Sajoo
      • New Life Long Learning article: “Ismaili Studies: A Summary Review” by Farhad Daftary.
      • Story about the Graduate Studies Department one-day conference entitled, “Literature and the South Asian Communities of East Africa” in conjunction with the Arts and Humanities Research Board (AHRB) Centre for Asian and African Literatures and the School of Oriental and African Studies' Centre of South Asian Studies.

      March 2003

      • Story about the translation of Farhad Daftary's A Short History of the Ismailis into French.
      • New Life Long Learning article: Keynote Address at ‘Intellectual Traditions in Islam’ Seminar,University of Cambridge by Aziz Esmail.
      • Story about Dr Nader El-Bizri speaking to 250 Lower Sixth Form students at Eton College (Windsor, UK) on the nature of Islam and the political world order.
      • New Life Long Learning article: “Nasir Khusraw: A Portrait of the Persian Poet, Traveller and Philsopher” from Nasir Khusraw, The Ruby of Badakhshan: A Portrait of the Persian Poet, Traveller and Philosopher by Alice Hunsburger.
      • Story about the conclusion of the Institute’s seminar series, “Muslim Pluralism: Historical and Contemporary Encounters with the Internal Others”
      • New Life Long Learning article: “Aga Khan III: A Study in Humanism” by K K Aziz

      February 2003

      • In Memoriam: Annemarie Schimmel, 1922-2003.
      • New Academic article : “Present-Day Islam Between Its Tradition and Globalisation” by Mohammed Arkoun.

      January 2003

      • Story about the Institute’s latest publication in the Ismaili Text and Translation Series: The Fatimids and their Successors in Yaman by Ayman Fu’ad Sayyid
      • New Life Long Learning article: “Introduction”, from The Assassin Legends: Myths of the Isma‘ilis by Farhad Daftary.
      • New Life Long Learning article: “The Quest for Excellence: Towards a Cultural Renaissance?” by Karim H. Karim.
