    Reading Lists: Shi'i Islam

    Shi‘i Islam

    Asfaruddin, Asma. Excellence and Precedence: Medieval Islamic Discourse on Legitimate Leadership. Leiden: Brill, 2002.

    Ajami, Fouad. The Vanished Imam: Musa al-Sadr and the Shia of Lebanon. Ithaca: Cornell University Press, 1986.

    ‘Ali ibn Abu Talib. Nahj ul-Balagha, 12th edition, tr. Askari Jafri. New York: Islamic Seminary Publications, 1999.

    Amir-Moezzi, Mohammad Ali. The Divine Guide in Early Shi‘ism: The Sources of Esotericism in Islam. Albany: State University of New York Press, 1994.

    Arjomand, Said. The Shadow of God and the Hidden Imam: Religion, Political Order and Societal Change in Shi‘ite Iran from the beginning to 1890. Chicago: University of Chicago Press, 1984.

    ——, ed. Authority and Political Culture in Shi‘ism. Albany: State University of New York Press, 1988.

    Ayoub, Mahmoud. Redemptive Suffering in Islam: A Study of the Devotional Aspects of ‘Ashura’ in Twelver Shi‘ism. The Hague: Mouton, 1978.

    Bayat, Mangol. Mysticism and Dissent: Socioreligious Thought in Qajar Iran. Syracuse: Syracuse University Press, 1982.

    Blank, Jonah. Mullahs on the Mainframe: Islam and Modernity Among the Daudi Bohras. Chicago: University of Chicago Press, 2001.

    Corbin, Henry. Spiritual Body and Celestial Earth: From Mazdean Iran to Shi‘ite Iran. Princeton: Bollingen, 1977.

    Chittick, William, ed. and tr. A Shi‘ite Anthology. London: Muhammadi Trust, 1980.

    Cole, Juan R. I. Roots of North Indian Shi‘ism in Iran and Iraq: Religion and State in Awadh, 1722-1859. Berkeley: University of California Press, 1988.

    Daftary, Farhad. The Isma‘ilis: Their History and Doctrines. Cambridge: Cambridge University Press, 1990.

    ——A Short History of the Ismailis: Traditions of a Muslim Community. Edinburgh: Edinburgh University Press, 1998.

    Engineer, Asghar Ali. The Bohras. Ghaziabad: Vikas, 1980.

    Farahani, Mirza. A Shi‘ite Pilgrimage to Mecca, 1885-1886: The Safarnameh of Mirza Mohammad Hosayn Farahani. Austin: University of Texas Press, 1990.

    Fischer, Michael. Iran: From Religious Dispute to Revolution. Cambridge: Harvard University Press, 1980.

    Fischer, Michael and Mehdi Abendi, Debating Muslims: Cultural Dialogues in Postmodernity and Tradition. Madison: University of Wisconsin Press, 1990.

    Fuller, Graham and Rend Francke. The Arab Shi‘a: The Forgotten Muslims. New York: St Martin’s Press, 1999.

    Halm, Heinz. Shiism. Edinburgh: Edinburgh University Press, 1991.

    ——Shi‘a Islam: From Religion to Revolution. Princeton: Markus Wiener, 1997.

    Jafri, S. Husain M. Origins and Early Development of Shi‘a Islam. London and New York: Longman, 1979.

    Khusraw, Nasir. Knowledge and Liberation: A Treatise on Philosophical Theology, tr. by Faquir Hunzai. London: I.B. Taurus in association with The Institute of Ismaili Studies, 1998.

    Lalani, Arzina. Early Shi‘a Thought: The Teachings of Imam Muhammad al-Baqir. London: I.B. Taurus in association with The Institute of Ismaili Studies, 2000.

    Lev, Yaacov. State and Society in Fatimid Egypt. Leiden: Brill, 1991.

    Loeffler, Reihold. Islam in Practice: Religious Beliefs in a Persian Village. Albany: State University of New York Press, 1988.

    Madelung, Wilferd. The Succession to Muhammad. Cambridge: Cambridge University Press, 1997.

    Makarem, Sami. The Political Doctrine of the Isma‘ilis: The Imamate. Delmar: Caravan, 1977.

    Modarressi, Hossein. Crisis and Consolidation in the Formative Period of Shi‘ite Islam: Abu Ja‘far ibn Qiba al-Razi and his Contribution to Imamite Shi‘ite Thought. Princeton: Darwin, 1993.

    Mottahedeh, Roy. The Mantle of the Prophet: Religion and Politics in Iran. New York: Simon and Schuster, 1985.

    Nanji, Azim Nanji. The Nizari Isma‘ili Tradition in the Indo-Pakistan Subcontinent. New York: Caravan, 1978.

    Nasr, S. H., Hamid Dabashi and S. Vali Reza Nasr. Shi‘i Islam: Doctrine, Thought, and Spirituality. Albany: State University of New York Press, 1988.

    Pinault, David. The Shiites: Ritual and Popular Piety in a Muslim Community. New York: St. Martin’s Press, 1992.

    Al-Qaddumi, Ghada. Book of Gifts and Rareties: Kitab al-Hadaya wa al-Tuhaf. Harvard: Center for Middle Eastern Studies, 1996.

    Sachedina, Abdulaziz. Islamic Messianism: The Idea of the Mahdi in Twelver Shi‘ism. Albany: State University of New York Press, 1981.

    Sanders, Paula. Ritual and the City in Fatimid Cairo. Albany: State University of New York Press, 1994.

    Sobhani, Ayatollah Ja‘far. Doctrines of Shi‘i Islam: A Compendium of Imami Beliefs and Practices, tr. And ed. Reza Shah-Kazemi. London: I. B. Tauris in association with The Institute of Ismaili Studies, 2001.

    Schubel, Vernon. Religious Performance in Contemporary Islam: Shi‘i Devotional Rituals in South Asia. Columbia, SC: University of South Carolina Press, 1993.

    Wiley, Joyce. The Islamic Movement of Iraqi Shi‘as. Boulder: Lynee Rienner, 1992.
